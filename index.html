GLOBAL HEART EQUITY INSTITUTE

GHEI is a global institute committed to improving cardiovascular health equity in low-resource settings through mobile PPG technology, scientific research, and community-centered education.

What We Do

  • • Mobile PPG & AI cardiovascular screening.
  • • Equity-focused global cardiovascular research.
  • • Low-literacy-friendly heart health education.
  • • Open science & global collaboration.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To improve global cardiovascular health equity through accessible mobile technology, rigorous scientific research, and community-centered education.

Vision

A world where every person—regardless of geography or income—has access to early detection and prevention of heart disease.

Why GHEI Exists

Global Burden

Cardiovascular disease remains the leading cause of early mortality worldwide.

Inequitable Access

Many communities lack reliable, affordable early-screening solutions.

Need for Low-Cost Innovation

High-cost digital tools widen health disparities—mobile PPG can change this.
